# Quality Criteria: Twitter Engagement

This checklist is used by **Rita Review** to evaluate each comment suggestion for **Growth Collective**.

| Criterion              | Goal       | Description                                                                 |
|----------------------|------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Lead Relevance       | 9/10+      | Is the author a decision-maker (Founder/CMO) of a DTC/E-com brand ($3-5M range)? |
| Tone of Voice        | 8/10+      | Is the tone a Strategic Partner (no emojis, consultative, peer-to-peer)? |
| Insight Strength     | 8/10+      | Does the comment add a real performance or strategy insight?            |
| Engagement (CTA)     | 7/10+      | Does the comment invite natural reflection or response (without forcing)? |
| Brand Safety         | 10/10      | Is there no "desperate salesperson" tone or controversial content?       |

## Verdict Rules
- **APPROVE:** Average >= 8/10 and no criterion below 6/10.
- **REJECT:** Average < 8/10.
- **REJECT (Critical):** Failure in tone of voice (presence of emojis) or aggressive commercial tone.
