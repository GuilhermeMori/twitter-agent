# Domain Framework: Twitter Strategic Engagement (Growth Collective)

This framework defines the monitoring and engagement process for DTC and e-commerce brands.

## 1. Monitoring (Research Agent)
- **Frequency:** 1 hour (scheduled) or on-demand.
- **Sources:** Profiles of DTC brand founders, e-commerce CMOs, and hashtags such as #DTC, #PerformanceMarketing, #Ecom growth.
- **Filters:** Decision-makers of brands with an estimated revenue of $3-5M.
- **Goal:** Collect posts where there are discussions about growth strategy, agency issues, or scaling challenges.

## 2. Copywriting (Creation Agent)
- **Input:** Original post + Growth Collective ICP.
- **Prompt:** Generate a "Strategic Partner" comment, in English, without emojis, focused on adding value or sharing a performance insight.
- **Rule:** Never post directly.

## 3. Review (Review Agent)
- **Checklist**: Lead relevance + Insight quality + Brand Safety (strategic tone).
- **Verdict**: APPROVED | REJECTED (with reason).

## 4. Human Approval (Gmail)
- **Email:** Send via Blotato to the Reviewer.
- **Retry:** If rejected, the creation agent must adjust to a more consultative and less aggressive commercial tone.
